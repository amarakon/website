Message

Despite being a programmer and enjoying it, I think software has gone too far. Too many of the applications we use today violate our basic human rights. They violate our privacy and freedom and use deceptive practices in an attempt to get people to justify it. I am not having it. My goal is to create software that is ethical and good. Did the developers for proprietary software companies not pay attention in university? Why are they creating awful software that hogs so much memory and doesn’t free it? I will never know.

Religion

I am a Shia Muslim. Islam is a religion of piece according to our holy text: the Qur’an. I will be working on a command-line tool that allows you to read the Qur’an from the command-line. Of course, it is still better to read from the actual book itself for obvious reasons. However, not everyone has access to it, as it is illegal in some countries. It is nice to combine two important aspects of my life to make something good.
